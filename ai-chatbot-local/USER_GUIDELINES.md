# 📘 Chin Hin Employee Assistant - User Guidelines

Welcome to the **Chin Hin Employee Assistant**! This AI-powered companion is designed to simplify your daily work life by helping you manage leave, book meeting rooms, raise IT support tickets, and much more—all through a natural chat interface.

---

## 🚀 How to Access

You can interact with the assistant in two ways:

1.  **Mobile App**: Download and open the **Employee Management** app on your phone (via Expo Go).
2.  **Web UI**: Access the internal portal at `https://tnt-bc5-chatbot-api.azurewebsites.net`.

---

## ✨ Key Features & How to Use

### 📅 Leave Management
Manage your time off without filling out complex forms.
- **Check Balance**: Ask "How many days of annual leave do I have left?"
- **Apply for Leave**: Say "I want to take leave from next Monday to Wednesday for a vacation."
- **Medical/Unpaid**: Specify the type, e.g., "Applying for 1 day of medical leave today."

### 🏢 Meeting Room Booking
Find and book a space for your next collaboration.
- **Browse Rooms**: Ask "What meeting rooms are available for tomorrow at 2 PM?"
- **Book a Room**: Say "Book Conference Room A for Friday at 10 AM for 1 hour."

### 🎫 IT Support & Ticketing
Get technical help quickly.
- **Raise a Ticket**: "My laptop is overheating, I need an IT ticket." or "I can't connect to the office Wi-Fi."
- **Check Status**: "What is the status of my ticket for the monitor issue?"

### 🧠 HR & Policy Assistant
Get answers from the employee handbook.
- **Policies**: "What is the company's policy on remote work?"
- **Benefits**: "Tell me about our health insurance coverage."

### 🚌 Other Services
- **Visitor Management**: "Pre-register a visitor named John Doe for tomorrow at 9 AM."
- **Wellness**: "Give me some tips for managing stress at work."
- **Training**: "What upcoming training sessions are available?"

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips for Better Interaction

- **Be Specific**: Instead of "Book a room," try "Book Room B for 2 PM today."
- **Natural Language**: You don't need to use rigid commands. Talk to the assistant like you're talking to a colleague.
- **Check Details**: The assistant will always confirm details with you before finalizing a booking or ticket.

---

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

- **"Unauthorized" Error**: If the app says you are unauthorized, please contact your administrator. (Admin: Run `.\refresh_token.ps1` in the backend folder).
- **Slow Response**: Since the assistant uses advanced AI, it may take a few seconds to process complex requests. Please be patient!
- **Incorrect Information**: If the assistant misunderstood you, simply say "No, I meant..." and it will correct itself.
